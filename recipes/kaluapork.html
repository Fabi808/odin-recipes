Kalua Pork

Description

Kalua Pork is a traditional Hawaiian dish made from slow cooked pork with Hawaiian salt.

Ingredients

  • Boneless pork butt
  • Hawaiian sea salt
  • Liquid smoke
  • Green cabbage (optional)

Cooking Directions

  1. Brown whole pork butt in a slow or instant cooker and leave it inside. Do not drain.
  2. Add in water, Hawaiian sea salt, and liquid smoke to pot. Cook for 90 minutes.
  3. Cut green cabbage into 2-inch squares. Add into kalua pork pot for the last 10 minutes of cooking.
  4. Once done cooking, shred the meat with a fork and serve hot with white rice.
