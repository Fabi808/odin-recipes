Lomi Salmon

Lomi Salmon is a traditional Hawaiian side dish consisting of salted salmon
and diced tomatoes, white onions, and green onions.

Ingredients

  • Salted salmon
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Green onion

Cooking Directions

  1. Dice the salted salmon, tomatoes, and onions into small eraser-size cubes. Put into a bowl.
  2. Thinly slice green onion and add to bowl.
  3. Gently mix ingredients together and you're done! Serve cold along with some meat and rice.
